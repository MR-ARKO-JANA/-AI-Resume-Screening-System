Getting Started

1

Create Account

Sign up with your email and create a secure password. You'll be taken to the dashboard after registration.

2

Upload Resume

Drag & drop or browse to upload candidate resumes in PDF, DOC, or DOCX format (max 5MB).

3

Add Job Description

Paste the job description or select from saved templates. The AI uses this to match skills.

4

AI Screening

Click "Start AI Screening" and our AI will analyze the resume, extract skills, and generate a match score.

5

View Results

See detailed scoring breakdown, skill match analysis, and AI recommendation for each candidate.

6

Take Action

Shortlist, reject, or schedule interviews. Export data as CSV for team collaboration.

Features Guide

AI-Powered Screening

Our AI engine analyzes resumes against job descriptions using three key metrics:

  • Skill Match (60%) — Technical and soft skills comparison
  • Keyword Match (30%) — JD keyword relevance scoring
  • Experience (10%) — Years and level assessment
Core Feature

Candidate Management

Manage all your candidates in one place with powerful tools:

  • Filter by status: Shortlisted, Pending, Rejected
  • Bulk actions: Select multiple and shortlist/reject
  • Export candidate data as CSV
  • Detailed profile view with GitHub & LinkedIn analysis
Management

Profile Lookup

Deep-dive into candidates' online profiles:

  • GitHub analysis: repos, stars, languages, commit activity
  • LinkedIn insights: experience, certifications, skills
  • AI-generated professional summary
  • Available directly from the candidate modal
New Feature

Jobs Portal

Browse and manage job openings:

  • Auto-synced job listings from database
  • Search by title, company, or skills
  • Filter by location
  • Apply with resume directly and get AI match score
Portal

Templates

Save time with reusable templates:

  • Email templates: Interview invites, rejections, offers
  • Job description templates for common roles
  • Screening form templates
  • AI-generated templates coming soon
Productivity

Customizable Scoring

Fine-tune AI scoring to your needs:

  • Adjust skill, keyword, and experience weights
  • Visual scoring chart preview
  • Save custom configurations per team
  • Total must equal 100% (validated)
